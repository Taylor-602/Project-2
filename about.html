About

We are a vibrant cat lounge and adoption center. We founded The Catio to be a haven for our rescue cats while providing a bright and joyful space for our loyal customers! All of our cats and kittens have been rescued by our partners at Nashville Cat Rescue. Each Le Chats kitty resident spends their days socializing with our guests during kitty time and our wide variety of events. This priceless enrichment allows the cats's personalities to blossom before they find forever homes. We proudly offer our beloved community of Nashville—both locals and tourists alike—a place to relax, play and adopt!

This cat cafe was oringally opened in 1986 as a rescue center for cats. The owners decided they wanted to take a step into a new lifestyle to open up a cat cafe with all the rescues they found. Many years later, Le Cafe du Chat is still open to this day!
